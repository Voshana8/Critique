HOW HAPPY ARE WE ?


What is world happiness and how is it measured


World happiness is measure through many tests carried out around the world. Every year the world health organization does a collective survey to measure the world happiness level all around the world.These surveys will look into many aspects of peoples lives. Financial growth, birth rates, life expectancy and the overall "Ladder Score".


What is "Ladder Score"


It asks respondents to think of a ladder, with the best possible life for them being a 10 and the worst possible life being a 0. Respondents would have to think about all asepcts of their life. Financial,Political and most importanly their overall health
